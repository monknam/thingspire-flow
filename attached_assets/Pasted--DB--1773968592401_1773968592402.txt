마스터 프롬프트
You are a senior full-stack product engineer and product architect.

Build a production-ready MVP web application called “Thingspire Flow”.

This product is the Phase 1 foundation of a larger integrated management system for a small company (around 30 employees). The company is going through organizational change, and the immediate goal is to run a trustworthy, structured organizational culture survey and provide management dashboards. The system must be designed so it can later expand into:
- 360 feedback
- goal management
- performance review
- national R&D project / task management
- AI Q&A insights layer

Do NOT build this as a toy survey app. Build it as the first module of a long-term internal operating system.

==================================================
1. PRODUCT CONTEXT
==================================================

Product name:
- Thingspire Flow

Core concept:
- A company operating system for managing the flow of organization, work, feedback, and culture.

Current Phase:
- Phase 1 only

Phase 1 scope:
- Microsoft login (Outlook / Microsoft 365 / Entra ID based login)
- user / department / role management
- organizational culture survey management
- survey participation flow for employees
- admin dashboard for aggregated results
- data model that supports future expansion

Future phases (do not fully implement now, but architect for them):
- Phase 3: AI Q&A over HR / survey summary data
- Phase 2: 360 feedback, performance review, goal management, project management

==================================================
2. COMPANY CONTEXT
==================================================

The company is a Korean company named Thingspire.
About 30 employees.
Many employees are in IT / development / planning-related roles.

Current business / organizational context:
- first time running an organization culture survey
- new CTO joined recently
- organizational culture and working style are changing
- there may be side effects / friction from this change
- a new head of management joined
- several long-time organization leaders have left
- the whole company, not just the CTO / development area, is experiencing change
- employees may feel uncertainty about the company’s future
- the company recently experienced deficit / financial pressure
- therefore this survey is not just a routine HR activity; it is a change-management and organizational diagnosis tool

This context should influence UX copy, admin interpretation, and survey framing.

==================================================
3. IMPORTANT PRODUCT PRINCIPLES
==================================================

This product must reflect these principles:

1) Trust first
- Employees must feel safe using it.
- The survey must be framed as an organizational diagnosis tool, not a personal evaluation tool.
- Strong anonymity guidance is required.

2) Organizational diagnosis, not just satisfaction
- The survey is meant to identify structural issues:
  - alignment
  - execution
  - collaboration
  - motivation
  - culture health
  - atmosphere / psychological safety

3) Small company reality
- Since the company has only around 30 employees, the system must avoid fake statistical sophistication.
- It should emphasize:
  - company-wide census logic, not sample logic
  - patterns and trends
  - qualitative insights
  - careful handling of small team anonymity

4) Long-term extensibility
- Data model, navigation, and architecture must be extensible to future modules.
- Do not hardcode this as a one-off survey site.

5) Professional internal SaaS feel
- Clean, calm, trustworthy UI
- feels like an internal operating system, not a generic form builder

==================================================
4. TECHNICAL PREFERENCES
==================================================

Preferred stack:
- Next.js
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Supabase for database and auth
- PostgreSQL
- Microsoft OAuth / Entra ID login
- charts for admin dashboard
- modular architecture

If some details need reasonable assumptions, choose stable internal-tool best practices.

==================================================
5. AUTHENTICATION / ACCESS CONTROL
==================================================

The company uses Outlook / Microsoft accounts.
Use Microsoft login, not Google login.

Authentication requirements:
- login via Microsoft account
- restrict access to company domain only
- create profile record on first login
- support roles:
  - admin
  - leader
  - member

Role behavior:
- admin:
  - full access
  - create and manage survey cycles
  - edit questions and survey intro pages
  - see company-wide dashboards
- leader:
  - can see limited dashboard for their scope
  - must NOT see identifiable employee survey responses
- member:
  - can log in
  - can see active survey
  - can answer survey
  - can see only their own submission status
  - no access to aggregated sensitive dashboards

==================================================
6. INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE / NAVIGATION
==================================================

Main layout should include:
- top bar
  - Thingspire Flow logo / title
  - user profile menu
  - placeholder global search area for future AI insights
- left sidebar navigation
  - Home / Dashboard
  - 조직문화 설문 (Organization Culture Survey)
  - Reports / Insights (Phase 1 limited)
  - Admin (only for admin)
  - future modules can appear as disabled/coming soon or be structurally prepared:
    - 다면평가
    - 목표관리
    - 성과평가
    - 과제관리

The UX should clearly feel like part of a broader management system.

==================================================
7. PHASE 1 CORE PAGES
==================================================

Build these pages/components:

A. Login page
- Microsoft login button
- simple internal product branding
- message that only company accounts can access

B. Main dashboard (member)
- current active survey
- participation status
- survey due date
- simple guidance

C. Survey list page
- list all survey cycles
- show status:
  - 진행중
  - 예정
  - 종료
- example item:
  - 2026년 1분기 조직문화 설문

D. Survey intro/detail page
This is very important.
Before entering the survey, show a proper explanation page with:
- survey title
- why the company is doing this survey
- what the company is trying to understand
- what organizational direction / culture the company aspires to
- theoretical background in short, plain language
- very explicit anonymity principle
- statement that personal identification / personal punitive use is prohibited
- guidance for how employees should answer
- CTA button: 설문 시작하기

E. Survey answering page
- section-based or step-based survey UX
- progress bar
- previous / next
- autosave or save draft
- submit button
- confirmation dialog on final submit

F. Survey completion page
- completion message
- thank-you copy
- reminder that results are used for organizational improvement

G. Admin survey management pages
- create survey cycle
- edit survey intro content
- upload/import questionnaire template
- minimal question editing
- activate / close survey

H. Admin results dashboard
- response rate
- section-level scores
- question-level average scores
- company-wide heatmap
- department-level view only when anonymity threshold is satisfied
- qualitative response summary area
- no personal identifiable result display

==================================================
8. SURVEY OPERATING MODEL
==================================================

Important design decision:
For Phase 1, do NOT build a complex no-code form builder.

Instead implement:
- questionnaire template import (CSV or structured seed)
- minimal editing in admin UI:
  - edit text
  - reorder sections/questions
  - activate/deactivate questions
  - edit intro texts
- draft / active / closed states

The survey system should support:
- Likert 5-scale questions
- long-text questions
- section grouping

==================================================
9. SURVEY DATA / ANONYMITY RULES
==================================================

Critical:
This is a trust-sensitive internal system.

Rules:
- Survey is for organizational diagnosis, not individual evaluation.
- Individual identifiable reporting is prohibited.
- Small group protection is required.
- Team/department level results should be shown only if response count reaches threshold.
- Default anonymity threshold: 5 responses
- If a team has fewer than threshold responses, hide that team-level result or roll up to a higher level.
- Raw qualitative comments should not be casually displayed with identifiable clues.
- Admin dashboard should prioritize summarized patterns, themes, and aggregate results.

This must be reflected both in UI and data logic.

==================================================
10. SURVEY PURPOSE STATEMENT
==================================================

Use this logic in the survey intro:

This survey is not intended to evaluate individuals.
It is intended to diagnose whether our organization is working effectively and healthily during a period of significant change.

The company wants to understand:
- whether direction is clear
- whether work is being executed effectively
- whether collaboration is functioning
- whether employees feel meaning, ownership, and motivation
- whether the organization is psychologically safe and healthy
- whether the general atmosphere of the company and teams is stable, energetic, and trustworthy

==================================================
11. REFERENCE-INFORMED SURVEY STRUCTURE
==================================================

This survey should be presented as being informed by well-known management / organizational references, but do not claim official licensed use.
It is “reference-informed” by ideas commonly associated with:
- psychological safety
- organizational health
- employee engagement
- change management

The survey should be organized into 6 sections.

Total questions:
- 40 quantitative questions
- 3 qualitative questions

Use Korean UI text for the actual survey content.

==================================================
12. FULL SURVEY QUESTIONNAIRE
==================================================

Use the following structure and wording.

------------------------------------------
SECTION 1. 방향성과 정렬
------------------------------------------

Section description:
우리는 같은 방향을 보고 있는가, 그리고 회사-팀-개인의 목표가 연결되어 있는가를 확인합니다.

Questions:
1. 나는 우리 회사의 전략과 목표를 이해하고 있다.
2. 나는 우리 팀의 목표를 명확히 알고 있다.
3. 나의 업무가 회사의 목표와 어떻게 연결되는지 알고 있다.
4. 회사는 중요한 우선순위를 명확하게 제시한다.
5. 현재 회사가 나아가는 방향은 적절하다고 생각한다.
6. 변화가 있을 때 회사는 그 배경과 방향을 충분히 설명한다.

------------------------------------------
SECTION 2. 실행력
------------------------------------------

Section description:
우리 조직이 실제로 일을 추진하고 실행하는 방식이 효과적인지 확인합니다.

Questions:
7. 우리 팀은 목표를 실제 실행으로 잘 연결한다.
8. 의사결정은 불필요하게 지연되지 않는다.
9. 우리 조직의 업무 프로세스는 효율적이다.
10. 문제 발생 시 우리 조직은 빠르게 인지하고 해결한다.
11. 업무 수행에 필요한 리소스(인력, 정보, 도구)는 적절히 제공된다.
12. 업무를 진행할 때 역할과 책임(R&R)이 비교적 명확하다.
13. 우리 조직은 변화에 맞추어 일하는 방식을 개선하려고 노력한다.

------------------------------------------
SECTION 3. 협업과 커뮤니케이션
------------------------------------------

Section description:
부서와 팀을 넘어서 정보가 공유되고 협업이 이루어지는지 확인합니다.

Questions:
14. 우리 팀 내 협업은 원활하게 이루어진다.
15. 부서 간 협업은 효과적으로 이루어진다.
16. 필요한 정보는 적시에 공유된다.
17. 업무 관련 피드백은 필요한 수준으로 이루어진다.
18. 중요한 변화나 결정사항은 관련자에게 충분히 공유된다.
19. 서로 다른 의견이 있을 때도 대화를 통해 조율하려는 분위기가 있다.
20. 업무 진행 상황과 이슈를 공유하는 문화가 비교적 잘 형성되어 있다.

------------------------------------------
SECTION 4. 몰입과 동기부여
------------------------------------------

Section description:
구성원들이 자율성과 의미를 느끼며 자발적으로 움직이고 있는지 확인합니다.

Questions:
21. 나는 내 업무에 의미를 느낀다.
22. 나는 맡은 업무 이상으로 기여하고 싶은 의욕이 있다.
23. 나는 내 업무를 수행할 충분한 권한을 가지고 있다.
24. 나는 새로운 시도나 개선 의견을 제안하도록 장려받는다.
25. 나의 역량과 강점이 업무에 잘 활용되고 있다고 느낀다.
26. 우리 회사에서 계속 일하고 싶은 마음이 있다.

------------------------------------------
SECTION 5. 조직 건강성
------------------------------------------

Section description:
신뢰, 공정성, 책임문화, 지속적 개선의 수준을 확인합니다.

Questions:
27. 우리 조직은 서로를 존중하는 문화를 가지고 있다.
28. 우리 조직은 책임 회피보다 문제 해결을 우선하는 편이다.
29. 조직 내 의사결정은 비교적 공정하게 이루어진다.
30. 정치적 요소나 불필요한 눈치보다 업무 목적이 더 중요하게 다루어진다.
31. 우리 조직은 지속적으로 개선하려는 노력을 하고 있다.
32. 리더들은 조직의 방향성과 실행을 안정적으로 이끌고 있다고 느낀다.
33. 우리 조직은 어려운 상황에서도 기본적인 신뢰를 유지하고 있다고 느낀다.

------------------------------------------
SECTION 6. 조직 분위기
------------------------------------------

Section description:
조직의 정서적 상태, 팀 분위기, 심리적 안전감, 소속감을 확인합니다.

Questions:
34. 우리 조직은 전반적으로 활기차고 에너지가 있다.
35. 구성원들은 일에 대해 전반적으로 긍정적인 태도를 가지고 있다.
36. 우리 조직의 분위기는 전반적으로 건강하다고 느낀다.
37. 우리 팀은 서로 협력하려는 분위기가 있다.
38. 나는 내 의견을 비교적 자유롭게 말할 수 있다.
39. 다른 의견을 제시해도 불이익이 없다고 느낀다.
40. 나는 우리 회사에 소속감을 느낀다.

------------------------------------------
QUALITATIVE QUESTIONS
------------------------------------------

41. 우리 조직문화의 가장 큰 강점은 무엇이라고 생각하십니까?
42. 현재 우리 조직문화에서 가장 우선적으로 개선이 필요한 부분은 무엇입니까?
43. 추가로 전하고 싶은 의견이 있다면 자유롭게 작성해주세요.

------------------------------------------
ANSWER SCALE
------------------------------------------

For quantitative questions use 5-point Likert scale:
- 매우 그렇다 (5)
- 그렇다 (4)
- 보통이다 (3)
- 그렇지 않다 (2)
- 전혀 그렇지 않다 (1)
