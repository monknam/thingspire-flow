You are a senior full-stack engineer, product architect, HRIS designer, and organizational analytics specialist.

Build an internal system called "Thingspire Flow".

This is NOT a toy survey app.
This is the Phase 1 foundation of a larger integrated operating system for organizational management, people data, and analytics.

==================================================
1. PRODUCT CONTEXT
==================================================

Product name:
- Thingspire Flow

Company context:
- Around 30 employees
- Many employees are in IT / planning / development roles
- The company is going through organizational change
- A new CTO has joined
- Several long-time leaders have left
- A new head of management has joined
- Employees may feel uncertainty
- The company has financial pressure and organizational instability
- This is the first organizational culture survey ever conducted in the company

Therefore, this product is both:
1. an organizational diagnosis tool
2. a long-term internal HR/management operating system

==================================================
2. PHASE 1 GOALS
==================================================

Phase 1 must include:
1. Microsoft login
2. user / organization / role structure
3. organizational culture survey
4. advanced analytics dashboard
5. HRIS module (employee master data + assignment history)
6. future-ready architecture for later modules

Future modules (prepare architecture, do not fully build now):
- 360 feedback
- performance review
- goal management
- project / national R&D task management
- AI Q&A insights layer

==================================================
3. CRITICAL PRODUCT PRINCIPLES
==================================================

1) Strong anonymity for survey data
- survey responses must never be used for individual evaluation
- identifiable individual survey answers must never be publicly exposed
- small-group anonymity rules must be enforced

2) Organizational diagnosis, not employee surveillance
- this is about understanding the condition of the organization
- not about judging individuals

3) Organization culture is co-created by employees
- survey intro and UX should clearly communicate this

4) Small-company reality
- do not overuse fake statistics
- focus on census logic, patterns, and actionable interpretation

5) HRIS and survey data must be separated safely
- people master data can exist in the same system
- but raw sensitive personal data must be stored separately and securely
- survey dashboards must never expose sensitive personal information

2. 3-Level Organization Hierarchy

- level 1

3. Assignment / Appointment History
Preserve history of:
- organization moves
- appointments
- title changes
- leadership assignments

4. Extended Employee Profile
Store:
- education
- school
- major
- degree
- degree year
- science/tech registration number if applicable
- qualifications summary
- career history
- resume files
- documents

The following fields are highly sensitive and must be stored separately in a secure restricted table:
- national ID
- payroll account
- home address
- emergency contact
- personal phone
- personal email

These must NEVER appear in survey dashboards or general admin analytics pages.
