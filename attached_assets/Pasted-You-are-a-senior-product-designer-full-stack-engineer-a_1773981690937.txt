==================================================

Do NOT design this as a generic survey analytics page.

Design it as a professional internal organizational diagnosis dashboard that helps:

- management office identify support requests and improvement backlog

- What is the current condition of the organization?
- Which areas are weak?

- What should leadership / management do next?

==================================================
3. CORE DASHBOARD PRINCIPLES
==================================================

1) Diagnosis over decoration
- charts are not enough
- every major view should help interpretation

2) Actionability
- dashboard should lead to follow-up actions

3) Small-company logic
- do not pretend statistical significance
- use census / pattern / trend logic
- avoid overclaiming
- protect anonymity strictly

4) Strong anonymity
- never expose identifiable individual response
- if a group has fewer than 5 responses, hide or roll up the result

==================================================

Build the dashboard as a multi-tab analytics experience, not a single flat page.

Use these tabs:

A. Executive Summary

D. 정성 분석
E. 액션 플래너

5. TAB A: EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
==================================================

Purpose:
Provide a CEO/Executive-level one-screen overview.

Must include:

1. Overall response rate
2. Overall organizational health snapshot
3. Top 3 strengths
4. Top 3 risks
5. Lowest scoring section
6. Largest gap between organizations (if anonymity-safe)

==================================================

Purpose:

Include:

2. Theme grouping

   - leadership

   - systems / tools / environment
   - atmosphere / psychological safety

   “경영관리실 요청”

   - work systems

   - tools
   - access / permissions

   “리더십 / 경영진 관련 제언”
