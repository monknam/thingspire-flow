You are a senior full-stack engineer building an internal HR/Org management tool called "Thingspire Flow".

Build Phase 1: Organizational Culture Survey System.

This system must prioritize:
1. Absolute anonymity
2. Organizational diagnosis (NOT evaluation)
3. Actionable insights for leadership

Target users:
- 30 employees
- Mostly IT planners/developers
- Organization is currently going through major changes

---

# CORE FEATURES

## 1. Authentication
- Use Microsoft OAuth (Outlook / Azure AD login)
- Only allow company domain users
- Store user basic info:
  - id
  - email
  - name
  - organization (team)
  - role (admin / user)

IMPORTANT:
- Survey responses must NOT be directly tied to user identity
- Store responses with anonymized tokens

---

## 2. Survey System

### Survey Type:
"2026 Q1 Organizational Culture Survey"

### Survey Intro Page (REQUIRED UI)
Must clearly show:

- Purpose: organizational diagnosis, not evaluation
- Strong anonymity guarantee
- This survey is NOT used for performance review
- Culture is co-created by employees

---

## 3. Survey Structure

Use 7 sections:

1. Direction & Strategy
2. Execution
3. Collaboration
4. Engagement & Motivation
5. Organizational Health
6. Team Climate & Psychological Safety
7. Work Environment & Systems

---

## 4. Question Format

Use 5-point Likert scale:

5 = Strongly Agree  
4 = Agree  
3 = Neutral  
2 = Disagree  
1 = Strongly Disagree  

---

## 5. Questions (IMPORTANT: USE EXACT TEXT)

### SECTION 1: Direction & Strategy

- I understand the company's direction and strategy.
- I understand how my work connects to company goals.
- Our organization is responding effectively to changes.
- Leadership clearly communicates priorities.

---

### SECTION 2: Execution

- Our team executes work effectively.
- Decision-making is timely.
- We focus on solving real problems, not just symptoms.
- Work priorities are clear.

---

### SECTION 3: Collaboration

- Collaboration between teams works well.
- Necessary information is shared effectively.
- There is little blame-shifting between teams.
- Feedback between teams is timely.

---

### SECTION 4: Engagement & Motivation

- I feel motivated to contribute beyond expectations.
- I feel proud to work at this company.
- I would recommend this company to others.
- My work feels meaningful.

---

### SECTION 5: Organizational Health

- Roles and responsibilities are clearly defined.
- Processes are well-structured.
- There is a culture of accountability.
- Leadership decisions are fair.

---

### SECTION 6: Team Climate

- I feel safe sharing my opinions.
- I can speak honestly about issues.
- Team atmosphere is positive.
- Conflicts are handled constructively.

---

### SECTION 7: Work Environment & Systems

- The tools and systems we use are efficient.
- I have proper access to necessary systems.
- Systems support collaboration effectively.
- Our work environment supports productivity.
- When issues arise, support is provided.

DO NOT mention specific tools like Outlook or Microsoft.

---

## 6. Open-ended Questions (VERY IMPORTANT)

Include ALL:

1. What are the strongest aspects of our organization?
2. What is the most serious problem currently?
3. Provide a specific example of that problem.
4. What should we change first to improve the organization?
5. What should leadership (including executives) improve?
6. What support or changes do you want from management (HR/operations)?
7. What systems/tools/environment cause inefficiency?

---

## 7. Anonymity Rules (CRITICAL)

- Do NOT show individual responses
- Only show aggregated data
- If group size < 5 → hide group data
- No admin can see individual answers

---

## 8. Dashboard (Admin)

Must include:

- Overall score by section
- Heatmap (red/yellow/green)
- Organization-level comparison (if safe)
- Keyword extraction from open text
- Top issues summary

---

## 9. UX Requirements

- Left sidebar navigation
- Main content area
- Survey intro → survey → completion flow

Menu:

- Dashboard
- Surveys
- Results
